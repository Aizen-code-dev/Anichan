DMCA Takedown Policy

Last Updated: November 2025

At AniChan, we respect the intellectual property rights of others and comply with the Digital Millennium Copyright Act (DMCA). We do not host, stream, or store any anime or manga media on our servers. All content, such as titles, images, and metadata, is fetched automatically from publicly available third-party APIs.
